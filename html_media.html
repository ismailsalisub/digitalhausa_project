Saka Bidiyo da Sauti Adding Audio & Video

HTML5 yana ba da damar saka bidiyo da sautin kai tsaye a cikin shafin ba tare da buƙatar Adobe Flash ba. Ana amfani da alamar <video> da <audio>. Kowane ɗaya yana da kayan shafi kamar src, controls, autoplay, da loop don sarrafa yadda midiya take aiki.

HTML5 allows embedding audio and video directly into webpages without needing Adobe Flash. Use the <video> and <audio> tags. Each has attributes like src, controls, autoplay, and loop to control how media behaves.

Misali / Example
<video width="400" controls>
  <source src="bidiyo.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Burauzarku ba ta goyon baya bidiyo.
</video>

<audio controls>
  <source src="wak.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
</audio>

Fahimtar Midiya:

  • <video>: Alamar saka bidiyo. Video element.
  • <audio>: Alamar saka sautin. Audio element.
  • controls: Yana nuna madannin kunna/tsayawa. Shows play/pause controls.
  • <source>: Yana ba da hanyoyi daban-daban na fayil. Provides multiple file formats.
