Hanyoyi (Links) HTML Links

HTML • Hanyoyi (Links) (HTML Links)

Mene ne Hanyoyi (Links)? What are HTML Links?

Hanyoyi (Links) sune abin da ke sa yanar gizo ta zama 'yanar gizo' — suna haɗa shafuka da juna. Ana amfani da alamar <a> tare da kayan shafi href don ƙirƙirar hanya. Hanyoyi suna iya kai ku zuwa wani shafi na waje, wani wuri a cikin wannan shafin, ko kuma buɗe imel.

Links are what make the web a 'web' — they connect pages to each other. The <a> tag with the href attribute creates a link. Links can take you to an external page, a location within the same page, or open an email.

Misali / Example
<a href="https://digitalhausa.com">Ziyarci DigitalHausa</a>

<a href="mailto:hello@digitalhausa.com">Aika Imel</a>

Fahimtar Hanyoyi:

  • href: Yana nuna inda hanya take kai. Specifies the link destination.
  • target='_blank': Buɗe shafi a cikin sabon tag. Opens link in a new tab.
  • mailto:: Haɗa zuwa aika imel kai tsaye. Creates an email link.
